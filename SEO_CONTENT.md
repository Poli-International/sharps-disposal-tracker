## What is Sharps Disposal Compliance Tracker?

The Sharps Disposal Compliance Tracker is a free web tool that helps tattoo and piercing studios log every sharps container disposal event. It records the pickup date, container size, cost, provider, and any notes. The tool calculates total disposal costs, average cost per pickup, and forecasts the next collection date based on historical intervals. All data is stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage, so no account or internet connection is required after the page loads.

## Who Should Use This

**Tattoo Studio Owners and Managers** who need to maintain compliance records for clinical waste disposal and track spending on sharps container services.

**Piercing Studio Operators** who generate sharps waste and need a simple log to demonstrate proper disposal practices during inspections.

**Compliance Officers and Health & Safety Leads** responsible for documenting biohazard waste disposal across multiple studio locations.

**Studio Administrators Managing Waste Contracts** who want to compare provider costs, monitor pickup frequency, and forecast future service needs.

## How to Use

**Step 1: Set the Disposal Date**
The date field automatically defaults to today's date. You can change it to any past or future date when the pickup occurred.

**Step 2: Select Container Size**
Choose from the dropdown: 0.7 L (small/chairside), 1 L, 2 L, 5 L, 8 L, or Multiple containers.

**Step 3: Enter Cost (Optional)**
Type the amount paid for this disposal. Use decimal format, for example 12.50.

**Step 4: Add Provider Information (Optional)**
Enter the waste management company name, such as Daniels Health or NHS DOOP.

**Step 5: Add Notes (Optional)**
Include collection reference numbers, site names, or any other details.

**Step 6: Log the Disposal Event**
Click the "Log Disposal" button to save the record. The table and summary dashboard update immediately.

**Step 7: Review Your Summary Dashboard**
The dashboard shows total disposals, total cost, average cost per pickup, and the forecasted next collection date.

**Step 8: Export or Clear Records**
Use the "Export CSV" button to download all records as a spreadsheet. Use "Clear All" to delete all records permanently.

## Use Case Examples

**Example 1: Small Tattoo Studio Monthly Pickups**
A single-artist studio logs a 2 L container pickup on 2025-01-15 with Daniels Health costing £15.00. They log another on 2025-02-12 costing £15.00. The tool calculates an average gap of 28 days and forecasts the next pickup around 2025-03-12.

**Example 2: High-Volume Piercing Studio Weekly Disposals**
A busy piercing studio logs four 5 L container pickups per month with NHS DOOP at £22.50 each. After three months, the tool shows 12 total disposals, total cost £270.00, average cost £22.50, and forecasts the next pickup based on the 7-day average interval.

**Example 3: Multi-Provider Cost Comparison**
A studio uses two providers and logs each with the provider name. They export the CSV to analyze which provider offers better pricing per container size and which has more consistent pickup scheduling.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: Is my data stored on a server?**
No. All disposal records are stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage. No data is sent to any server.

**Q2: Can I access my records from a different device?**
No. Because data is stored locally, records are only available on the device and browser where you entered them. Export your CSV to transfer data between devices.

**Q3: How does the forecast date work?**
The tool calculates the average number of days between all your logged disposal events, then adds that average to the most recent disposal date.

**Q4: What happens if I clear my browser data?**
Clearing your browser cache, cookies, or site data will delete all your disposal records. Export your CSV regularly to keep a backup.

**Q5: Can I edit a record after I log it?**
The current version does not support editing. You can delete a record and re-enter it with corrected information.

**Q6: What container sizes are available?**
The dropdown includes 0.7 L, 1 L, 2 L, 5 L, 8 L, and Multiple containers.

**Q7: Is the cost field required?**
No. Cost is optional. Records without cost are still counted in total disposals but excluded from cost calculations.

**Q8: Can I export my data?**
Yes. Click "Export CSV" to download a spreadsheet file with all your records including date, container size, cost, provider, and notes.

**Q9: How many records can I store?**
There is no hard limit, but browser storage capacity varies. For typical studio use, thousands of records can be stored without issues.

**Q10: Does this tool work offline?**
Yes. After the page loads once, the tool works offline because all data processing and storage happens in your browser.
